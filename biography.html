Jinming Xu– Biography

Jinming Xu received his Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degree in mechanical engineering from Shandong University, China, in 2009 and PhD degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2016. He is now working with Prof Angelia Nedich and Prof Anna Scaglione as a postdoctoral scholar in the Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering. His current research interests focus on

  • Distributed optimization,

  • Cyber security of networked systems,

  • Large-scale signal processing and machine learning.

Academic Employment

  • Postdoctoral Scholar, Arizona State University, Phoenix, USA, 10/2017-Present.

  • Research Fellow, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 8/2016-9/2017.

  • Project Officer, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 8/2015-7/2016.

  • Research Assistant, Zhejiang University, China, 9/2009-8/2011.
