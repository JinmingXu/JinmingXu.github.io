Jinming Xu– Biography

Jinming Xu received his Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degree in mechanical engineering from Shandong University, China, in 2009 and PhD degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2016. His current research interests focus on

  • Distributed optimization,

  • Energy optimization for large-scale networked systems,

  • Large-scale signal processing and machine learning.

Academic Employment

  • Research Fellow, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 8/2016-Present.

  • Project Officer, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 8/2015-8/2016.

  • Research Assistant, Zhejiang University, China, 9/2009-8/2011.
