What's New!

  • Apr. 2020: I gave a talk (in Chinese) on “When do distributed optimization algorithms meet centralized counterparts and beyond”, Online.

    • In this talk, I introduce two new kinds of distributed optimization algorithms (i.e., distributed gradient-tracking methods and primal-dual methods) we recently developed that can converge much fast than other existing ones, and show their close connections; After that, I also show how these proposed algorithms can be unified in a proper way and that under what conditions they will have comparable performance with centralized counterparts.
